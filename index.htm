About Me

I'm Dmytro Hovenko, an aspiring FullStack Developer currently learning the fundamentals of web development and programming. I am passionate about technology and eager to build a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development.

My learning journey includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, PHP, Laravel, C, C++, C#, MySQL, MSSQL, and MongoDB. I am committed to continuous improvement and excited to apply my knowledge to real-world projects in the future.
